Cycling is a great sport to do as a proffesion for fitness and health or just for fun !! Some forms of cylcing racing include...

Road Cycling - Riding on paved roads at high speeds competitively with other riders. Mountain Biking - Off road cycling on rough terrain includong gravel, hills, mud/dirt paths and grass Track cycling - Racing on a velodrome a oval shaped track in a competitive environment. Cyclocross - A mix of road cycling and mountian biking, typically ridden on a cross country circuit with obstacles and a varied terrain.
